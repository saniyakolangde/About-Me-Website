Why paint?

W e all have a demanding routine, and every once in a while would love to escape the rut of our daily lives. Or when we yearn for something exciting and different, crave new challenges. Painting is the ultimate therapy!

One of the reasons I love painting is that it exposes us the world in a way that changes the way we look at it, it gives a whole new perspective.
