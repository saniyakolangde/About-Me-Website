Why read?

W e all have a demanding routine, and every once in a while would love to escape the rut of our daily lives. Or when we yearn for something exciting and different, crave new experiences but jusy do not find the time. Reading is the ultimate therapy!

One of the reasons I love reading is because it exposes us to this world of fantasies that enhance the way we think. It allows a way to escape and just broaden your perspectives.
