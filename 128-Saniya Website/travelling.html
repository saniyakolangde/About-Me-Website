Why travel?

W e all have a demanding routine, and every once in a while would love to escape the rut of our daily lives. Or when we yearn for something exciting and different, crave new experiences and new challenges. Travelling is the ultimate therapy!

One of the reasons I love travelling is that it exposes us the world in a way that changes the way we think, the way we see the world, the way we feel about ourselves.
